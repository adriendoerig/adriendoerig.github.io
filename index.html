About

I am a Guest Professor at Freie Universität Berlin , also affiliated with the Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience . In our research group, we combine cognitive neuroscience, AI and philosophy to understand the human mind, with a focus on vision.

Before moving to Berlin, I worked as a postdoc with Tim Kietzmann at the University of Osnabrück (DE) and the Donders Institute (NL). I obtained my PhD from EPFL (CH) under the supervision of Michael Herzog. During my undergrad, I studied life sciences, physics, and neuroscience.

I am interested in supervising all sorts of projects, at all sorts of levels (even though spots are unfortunately limited). Have a look at my research and publications, and feel free to contact me if you feel like we could be a good match.
